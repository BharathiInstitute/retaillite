✨ Features

Everything Your Shop Needs

Six powerful modules working together to make your business simpler, faster, and more profitable.

🛒

POS Billing

Lightning-fast checkout with barcode scanner, voice input, and smart search. Bill in seconds, not minutes.

📦

Inventory

Track every product, variant, and stock level. Get low-stock alerts. Import from CSV in one click.

📒

Khata Ledger

Digital udhar book — never forget a payment. Track credit, send reminders, maintain relationships.

📊

Reports & Analytics

See your sales at a glance — daily, weekly, monthly. Know your top products and trends.

Offline First

Works without internet. No network? No problem. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.

Thermal Printing

Print receipts instantly via Bluetooth thermal printer. 58mm paper, auto-print, GST-compliant bills.
