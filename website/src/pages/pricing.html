❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Free plan really free forever? +
Yes! The Free plan has no time limit. Use it as long as you want with up to 50 bills/month, 100 products, and 10 customers. No credit card required.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade from Free to Pro or Business anytime from the app. If you downgrade, your current billing cycle continues until it expires, then the new plan starts.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, and Net Banking through Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway.
What happens when I hit my bill limit? +
You'll see a friendly reminder to upgrade. Your existing data is always safe. You just can't create new bills until the next month or until you upgrade.
Is my data safe? +
Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We never share or sell your data.
Do I need internet to use the app? +
The app works offline for billing. Data syncs automatically when you're back online. All plans include offline mode.
